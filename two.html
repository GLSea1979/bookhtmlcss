Mashed Potatoes main page

  1. Chop potatoes into quarters
  2. Bring to a boil in salted water then reduce heat
  3. Simmer 15-20 minutes until tender
  4. Heat milk, butter and nutmeg
  5. Drain potatoes and mash
  6. Mix in milk mixture

Ingredients

  • 2lbs King Edward potatoes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
